Starset - Satellite

"Satellite" is the standout single from American rock band Starset's second album. This track a signature example of the themes Starset commonly use such as longing, guidance, and the human need for connection amidst isolation. Released as a single in August 2017, "Satellite" reached number 12 on the Billboard Mainstream Rock Songs chart.
